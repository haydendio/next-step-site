Our Process – What to Expect From Your Home Inspection

At Next Step Home Inspections, we believe in a seamless, stress-free inspection process. From scheduling to receiving your report, our streamlined process ensures you get the insights you need with no surprises or hidden fees.

Step 1: Schedule Your Inspection

✅ Flexible Appointments: We offer weekend and evening slots for your convenience.

✅ Service Area Coverage: We inspect homes throughout St. Louis, Jefferson County, and surrounding areas.

Step 2: Pay Upfront to Secure Your Appointment

💳 Why Do We Require Pre-Payment?

  • Guarantees your time slot, preventing last-minute cancellations.
  • Allows us to dedicate more time to each inspection.
  • Secure payment processing for hassle-free transactions.

💡 No Hidden Fees – What You Pay Is What You Get!

Step 3: Read & Sign the Standards of Practice Agreement

📖 Why This Step Matters:

  • Ensures you understand what’s included and what’s not in the inspection.
  • Outlines the scope of work, limitations, and exclusions to maintain transparency.
  • Helps set clear expectations for buyers, sellers, and agents.

📑 Download Our Standards of Practice Here

Step 4: Day of Inspection – What to Expect

🔍 We Perform a Thorough Home Evaluation, Including:

  • Exterior & Structural Integrity: Foundation, siding, grading, roof, and drainage.
  • Major Systems: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, water heaters, and appliances.
  • Interior Features: Windows, doors, and an Infrared camera inspection.

👥 Who Should Attend?

  • Buyers, sellers, and agents are encouraged to be present for real-time insights.
  • If you can’t attend, don’t worry! Your detailed report will include everything you need.

📞 Inspector Confirmation: We’ll call to confirm before arriving at the property.

Step 5: Receive Your Inspection Report Within 24 Hours

📩 Delivered Straight to Your Inbox

  • High-resolution photos of any issues found.
  • Easy-to-read summary with key findings.
  • Recommendations for repairs or further evaluation.

💡 Need Clarifications? We offer free post-inspection support to answer any questions you may have.
