Our Process – What to Expect From Your Home Inspection

At Next Step Home Inspections, we believe in a seamless, stress-free inspection process. From scheduling to receiving your report, our streamlined process ensures you get the insights you need with no surprises or hidden fees.

Step 1: Schedule Your Inspection

✅ Flexible Appointments: Weekend and evening slots available.

✅ Service Area: St. Louis, Jefferson County, and surrounding areas.

Step 2: Pay Upfront to Secure Your Appointment

💳 Why Pre-Payment?

  • Secures your time slot and reduces no-shows.
  • Allows more focus per client.
  • Secure, hassle-free payment process.

💡 No Hidden Fees – What You Pay Is What You Get!

Step 3: Sign the Standards of Practice Agreement

📖 Why This Matters:

  • Clarifies what is and isn’t covered in your inspection.
  • Outlines scope, limitations, and exclusions.
  • Sets expectations for all parties.

📑 Download available on our website.

Step 4: Day of Inspection – What to Expect

🔍 What We Inspect:

  • Exterior: foundation, roof, siding, grading, drainage.
  • Major Systems: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, water heaters.
  • Interior: windows, doors, infrared scan included.

👥 Attendance: Buyers, sellers, and agents are encouraged to attend. If not, our report will cover it all.

📞 We'll confirm with a call prior to arrival.

Step 5: Receive Your Report Within 24 Hours

📩 Delivery: Sent via email.

  • Includes photos of key issues.
  • Summarized findings for easy review.
  • Recommendations for follow-up.

💡 Free post-inspection support included.
