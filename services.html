New Build Inspection

🏡 Think new homes don’t have issues? Think again!

A New Build Inspection ensures that your brand-new home meets structural, electrical, and mechanical standards before you move in. Even professional builders can make mistakes, and early detection can save you from costly repairs.

What’s Included?

🛠 Foundation & Framing Checks – Ensuring the structural base is solid before walls go up.

🚰 Plumbing & Electrical Verification – Identifying installation errors before drywall covers them.

✅ Final Walkthrough Evaluation – Checking all systems and finishes before move-in.

🔹 Avoid major headaches later—get your new home inspected before closing!

11-Month Warranty Inspection

📆 Is Your Builder’s Warranty About to Expire?

Many builders provide a 1-year warranty on new homes. An 11-Month Warranty Inspection helps homeowners identify defects while the builder is still responsible for repairs.

What We Inspect:

✔ Structural Issues – Foundation cracks, roof defects, and settling problems.

✔ Major Systems – Plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and appliances.

✔ Leaks, Drainage, and Insulation Problems – Avoid moisture issues before they get worse.

📝 Includes a full report to present to your builder for potential warranty-covered repairs.

Pre-Drywall Inspection

🚧 Catch Construction Mistakes Before It’s Too Late!

A Pre-Drywall Inspection occurs before insulation and drywall are installed, ensuring your home’s framework, wiring, and plumbing are done correctly.

Why is This Important?

  • Structural Issues can be identified before they’re covered up.
  • Electrical & Plumbing Mistakes can be fixed without tearing into walls later.
  • Code Compliance & Safety Assurance ensure that everything is built to standard.

💡 Saves homeowners money by catching problems before finishing construction.

Sewer Scope Inspection

🛑 Hidden Sewer Line Issues Can Cost Thousands!

A Sewer Scope Inspection uses advanced high-resolution camera equipment to inspect the underground sewer system, checking for:

✔ Blockages & Root Intrusion – Tree roots can destroy pipes over time.

✔ Pipe Deterioration & Cracks – Older homes may have outdated clay or cast-iron pipes.

✔ Drainage Problems – Avoid surprise backups after move-in.

🔹 Recommended for ALL homebuyers—especially for homes 20+ years old.

Radon Inspection

☢ What is Radon?

  • A colorless, odorless radioactive gas that can cause lung cancer.
  • The EPA recommends ALL homes be tested regardless of location.

Our Radon Testing Process:

✅ 48-hour continuous radon test for accurate results.

✅ Professional-grade detection equipment to measure levels.

✅ Recommendations for mitigation if high levels are found.

💡 Protect your family’s health—schedule a radon test today!

Termite Inspection

🛑 Protect Your Home from Costly Termite Damage!

Termites cause billions of dollars in structural damage each year. If left unchecked, they can silently destroy your home’s wood framing, flooring, and support structures.

What We Check For:

✔ Active Infestations – Detecting live termites before they spread.

✔ Mud Tubes & Wood Damage – Common signs of an ongoing infestation.

✔ Past Infestation Evidence – Identifying whether previous damage is still a concern.

💡 Early detection saves thousands in repair costs.
